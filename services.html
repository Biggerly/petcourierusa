Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What's included in your transport service?
Our transport service includes climate-controlled vehicles, certified handlers, GPS tracking, regular rest stops, hydration, feeding as needed, and door-to-door delivery. All pets are monitored 24/7 during transit.
How do you ensure pet safety during transport?
Safety is our top priority. We use climate-controlled vehicles, certified pet handlers trained in pet first aid, real-time GPS tracking, regular wellness checks, and maintain constant communication with our transport teams.
What documentation is required for transport?
We handle all documentation including health certificates, vaccination records, and interstate travel permits. Requirements vary by state, but we manage everything to ensure compliance.
Can I track my pet's journey?
Yes! All our transports include real-time GPS tracking. You'll receive regular updates via email or SMS, and can access a live tracking portal to see your pet's location anytime.
What is your health guarantee?
We offer a 30-day comprehensive health guarantee for all pets. This covers any health issues that arise within 30 days of delivery, with 24/7 veterinary support and coverage for related expenses.
