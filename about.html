About PetCourier USA

Connecting loving families with healthy pets through safe, reliable transportation across the United States.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, PetCourier USA began with a simple mission: to make pet adoption and transport safe, accessible, and stress-free for families across the country.

What started as a small Georgia-based service has grown into a nationwide network, connecting verified breeders with loving homes through insured, climate-controlled transportation.

Today, we've helped thousands of families welcome new pets into their homes, maintaining a 100% safety record and earning trust through transparency and exceptional care.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable pet transportation services that prioritize animal welfare, customer satisfaction, and ethical breeding practices.

We believe every pet deserves a loving home, and every family deserves a stress-free experience when bringing home their new companion.

Our Values

Safety First

Every transport includes climate control, GPS tracking, and certified handlers.

Animal Welfare

We only work with USDA-licensed breeders who meet our ethical standards.

Excellence

Committed to delivering exceptional service at every step of the process.

Transparency

Clear communication, honest pricing, and open processes.
