Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our service

How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by distance. Typically 1-3 days for regional deliveries and 3-7 days for cross-country transport. You'll receive real-time tracking updates throughout the journey.
What safety measures do you have during transport?
Our vehicles are climate-controlled with GPS tracking, regular rest stops, and 24/7 monitoring. All pets travel with certified handlers trained in pet first aid and CPR.
Do you offer health guarantees?
Yes! All pets come with a 30-day health guarantee from licensed veterinarians. We also provide complete vaccination records and health certificates.
Can I visit the breeder before purchase?
Absolutely! We encourage and can arrange visits to our verified breeders. Virtual tours are also available if distance is a concern.
